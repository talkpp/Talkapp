About TalkApp Pro

TalkApp Pro
Version 2.0 · Build 2024

TalkApp Pro is a fast, secure, and completely private messaging platform. Built with modern technology to ensure your conversations stay between you and the person you're talking to — no ads, no tracking, no compromises.

🔒 Security & Privacy
🔐

Google OAuth 2.0 Authentication

We use official Google sign-in via Firebase. Your credentials are handled entirely by Google — we never see your password.

🚫

Zero Password Storage

TalkApp Pro does not store, transmit, or process any passwords. Authentication is fully delegated to Google's secure infrastructure.

📡

Firebase Secure Backend

All data is stored on Google Firebase with security rules. Your messages are protected by Google Cloud's enterprise-grade security.

🛡️

No Ads, No Tracking

We do not run ads, sell your data, or track your behaviour. What you talk about is your business, not ours.

Privacy Policy

🔒 Secure 🚫 No Ads 🛡️ No Tracking 🚫 No Password Stored

Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected.

📋 What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo URL from your Google account. We also store your chosen username, your list of conversations, and messages you send.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process your Google password — ever. We do not store payment information, device identifiers, or browsing history. We do not run analytics or third-party ad trackers.

🔐 Authentication Security

Authentication is handled entirely by Google Firebase OAuth 2.0. When you tap "Continue with Google," you are redirected to Google's secure login page. Your password is entered directly with Google — TalkApp Pro never sees, receives, or stores it at any point.

🗄️ Data Storage

All data is stored on Google Cloud Firestore, protected by Firebase Security Rules. Only authenticated users can access their own data. Messages are accessible only to the two participants in a conversation.

📤 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for any purpose. Your data is used solely to provide the TalkApp Pro messaging service.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your messages, username, and profile data will be permanently removed from our servers.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

TalkApp Pro uses browser local storage only to maintain your authenticated session via Firebase. No tracking cookies are used. No third-party cookies are set.

📬 Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please reach out through the app's About section or contact the developer directly.
